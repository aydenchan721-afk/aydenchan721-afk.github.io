Page Title Why Zero Waste?

Only 11% of the plastic used in Canada is recycled. That means nearly 90% ends up in landfills, incinerators, or the environment (including the oceans and Great Lakes). Despite growing concerns about plastic pollution, Canada doesn't have a national framework for managing plastics. (Canada's Plastic Pollution Problem: Report) We believe that a solution exists, and it starts with each individual. Collectively, we can make a change. Rethink: Purchase products made from reused, recycled or sustainably-harvested, non-toxic materials. Support systems where product manufacturers consider the full life-cycle of their product. Reduce: Plan consumption, and implement sustainable purchasing that supports social and environmental objectives, as well as local markets where possible. Reuse: Maximize reuse of materials and products. Maintain, repair or refurbish to retain value, usefulness, and function. (Source: Zero Waste Canada) Every little bit counts.

Our Mission

Earth Market is dedicated to providing the community with socially responsible solutions to everyday purchases. We aim to reduce waste by providing products with minimal packaging, and sourcing locally in Ontario or Canada wherever possible.

Meet the Owner

I live in a two-person household. I realized we were producing too much waste when we could easily fill two recycling bins, and a garbage bin every week or two. I actively began searching for low waste solutions to the products that I purchased regularly (namely household and groceries). Sourcing low waste essentials locally was more challenging than I anticipated. Recognizing a need, and after many months of research and hard work, Earth Market was born.
